Blurb

Decentology simplifies blockchain application development for enterprises

Blockchain application development is time-consuming and costly for businesses. Lack of mature tools and an ever-changing landscape is frustrating for developers. Decentology solves these problems by providing a suite of developer tools, the first of which is “DappStarter,” a SaaS platform that reduces friction and accelerates development by generating full-stack blockchain application source code.

Highlights:

  • Works with all public and private blockchains
  • Easy integration with existing business systems
  • SaaS freemium business model
  • Beta launched and gaining fast traction
  • Strong team with significant product and blockchain experience
